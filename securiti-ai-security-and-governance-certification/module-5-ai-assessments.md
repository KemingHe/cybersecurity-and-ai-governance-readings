# Comparing Various Types of AI Assessments

## Types of AI Assessments

The following assessments are ordered by a typical risk management lifecycle, progressing from organizational preparedness through internal evaluation to external third-party analysis.

| Concept | Explanation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Readiness Assessments** | Readiness assessments evaluate whether an organization has undertaken the right measures (i.e., administrative, legal, and technical) to comply with specific AI regulations or frameworks and all its current AI capabilities. |
| **Risk Assessment** | An AI assessment is an organization-wide exercise for the identification, analysis and treatment of threats and vulnerabilities within the organization's AI system. |
| **Impact Assessment** | Identification, analysis and evaluation of a particular use case of the AI systems, and its implications, both positive and negative, for people and their environment. |
| **Vendor AI Risk Assessments** | AI Vendor risk assessment is a process used by companies to evaluate the risks they may encounter when working with third parties, such as AI vendors, suppliers, contractors, or other business partners. |

## Risk Assessment vs. Risk Management

Risk assessment is a subset of risk management. Risk Management is the combined efforts to identify, assess and prioritize risks, and the subsequent application of resources to minimize, monitor and control the likelihood of unintended events arising from the risks.
